The Earth

About Earth

Venus is the second planet from the Sun. Earth is the only place in the universe where life and surface liquid water are known to exist. Earth's atmosphere contains 78% nitrogen and 21% oxygen, which is the result of the presence of life. The planet has a complex climate and weather system, with conditions differing drastically between climate regions. The solid surface of Earth is dominated by green vegetation, deserts and white ice sheets.

  • Diameter: 12,742 km
  • Orbit: 1 AU from the Sun (149.6 million km)
  • Interesting Fact: Earth's rotation is slowing down over time. In about 140 million years, a day on Earth will be 25 hours long instead of 24.
